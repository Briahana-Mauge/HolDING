About

holDING is a reference to a "Holding Space" - a therapy term for a private physical or digital place where someone can work out their feelings while being both encouraged and expected to be better, but not in the moment judged.

REFERENCE: http://efttherapycenter.com/holding-space-for-someone/

holDING is, by design, a micro social media site. A private place with limited access and reach. holDING is a place where you can stay in touch with that casually racist aunt of yours that shares Minion memes from the worst Facebook groups - without the stigma of socially interacting with her. That’s because holDING is specifically NOT an open wide-reaching platform like Facebook or Twitter or YouTube or the like. It’s a place where users can interact with others without being bombarded with outrage inducing headlines and ire fueled trending topics. A place where the members of a close knit micro community can be anonymous (if they so choose) but no one is discredited, shamed, or dehumanized. No one is performing for a world-wide audience, and no one has to fear being seen by a worldwide audience.

holDING takes the inherent isolation out of social media.

Because with holDING, you’ll never miss a DING.
